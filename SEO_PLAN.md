# SEO Plan

## Objective

Strengthen local visibility for searches around Abyssin cats, adoption intent, and the Morbihan / Saint-Ave / Vannes area.

## Important note

No one can guarantee a number-one Google ranking. What can be improved reliably is:

- technical SEO quality
- crawlability
- internal linking
- content depth
- local relevance

## Content rules

- Write for humans first.
- Keep one main search intent per page.
- Use the target phrase in the title, H1, intro, and naturally in body text.
- Add internal links to cats, contact, and related guides.
- Prefer real, precise wording over generic SEO filler.

## Image SEO

- Always upload a main image for each public cat profile.
- Use descriptive alt text.
- Keep images compressed and clean.

## Local SEO checklist

- Keep `APP_URL`, canonical URLs, and sitemap aligned with the main domain.
- Maintain accurate contact information across the site.
- Mention Saint-Ave, Vannes, Morbihan, and Bretagne naturally where relevant.
- Create or optimize a Google Business Profile if available.

## Monitoring

- Track impressions and queries in Google Search Console.
- Monitor indexed pages with `site:chatteriedessoleilsdorient.com`.
- Review clicks and titles every month.
